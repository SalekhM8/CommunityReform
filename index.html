Our Programmes

Comprehensive services designed to support, educate, and empower our community

Get Active

Programmes that enable and empower local people to get active.

Women's Boxing Club

In collaboration with WBC
Standard Operations
  • 3× group sessions per week
  • Ages 13+ • Female only
  • Boxing self-defence & sparring
Personal Training
  • Ages 10+ • Fitness and Boxing
  • Boxercise qualified & Level 3 PT
  • Therapy sessions (Level 3 counselling)

Community Centre

A welcoming space for everyone in our community.

  • Warm Hubs Keep warm and share food with our community
  • Food Bank Support In collaboration with Birmingham City Council
  • Celebrations Affordable venue for community events
  • Social Groups Connect with neighbors
  • Elderly Support Dedicated social clubs for seniors

Youth Clubs

Safe, engaging spaces for young people to thrive.

  • Open Access Youth Clubs Separate clubs for boys and girls
  • Specialist Youth Clubs STEM programmes and more
  • Personal Development Mentorship with professional experts
  • Targeted Support Specialist programmes for at-risk youth

Training & Education

Building skills and knowledge for a brighter future.

  • Community Based Learning Locally accessible education
  • English Lessons Language groups and support
  • Key Skills Maths and English fundamentals
  • Homework Clubs Academic support for students
  • Arts & Media Training Creative skills development

Information, Advice & Guidance

Expert support when you need it most.

  • Trusted Partnership Working with BVSC
  • Household Support Fund Official partner providing financial assistance
  • Housing Partnership With Refugee and Migrant Centre (RMC)
  • Translation Services Arabic to English document translation

Our Impact

Making a difference in our community since 2022

2022

Foundation

Community Reform CIC established with a vision to empower and support local communities through comprehensive programmes and services.

2023

Expansion

Launched Women's Boxing Club in partnership with WBC, established warm hubs with Birmingham City Council, and developed youth club programmes.

2024

Growth

Expanded education programmes, formed partnerships with BVSC and RMC, and became a Household Support Fund partner serving hundreds of community members.

2025

Future

Continuing to grow our impact with new programmes, deeper partnerships, and expanded services to support our thriving community.
